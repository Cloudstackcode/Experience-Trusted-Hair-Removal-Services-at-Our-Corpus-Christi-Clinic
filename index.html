Common Questions About Hair Removal Services

We’ve heard just about every question there is when it comes to laser hair removal. Here are a few of the most common ones we get from clients in Corpus Christi:

How many sessions will I need?

This depends on your hair and skin type, but most people see significant results after 6 to 8 sessions. Some may need touch-ups later on, depending on how their body responds.

Does it hurt?

Most people describe the feeling as a light snap against the skin—similar to a rubber band. The discomfort is brief, and we adjust the settings based on your sensitivity.

Is it permanent?

Laser hair removal provides long-term results, but it’s more accurate to say that it offers permanent hair reduction. While some hair may regrow, it’s usually finer and lighter. Many clients remain hair-free for months or even years.

Can I treat any area?

We offer treatments for a wide range of areas, including the face, underarms, legs, back, bikini line, and more.

Is it safe for all skin tones?

Yes—our technology and technician training allow us to work safely with a variety of skin tones. During your consultation, we’ll assess your specific needs to ensure the best results.
